What is fiber internet?

Fiber internet is a type of broadband connection that uses light and glass cables to transmit data signals to deliver an internet connection. This method of transmitting data results in fiber internet being the fastest and most reliable internet type available.

Fiber internet providers are almost always the fastest option because they provide symmetrical upload and download speeds, ranging from 100 Mbps to 10+ Gbps. Fiber is also one of the most expensive internet technologies but has the least latency issues.
